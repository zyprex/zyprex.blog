Header¶

head2

head3

Text¶

text emphasis can be italic or bold or italic bold or strikethrough

 _italic_ or __bold__ or ___italic bold___ ~~strikethrough~~
 *italic* or **bold** or ***italic bold***

Lists¶

ordered¶

numbered lists:

  1. One
  2. Two
  3. Three
1. One
1. Two
1. Three

unordered¶

each lists item start with ‘*’ or ‘-’ or ‘+’ and a space:

  • One
  • Two
  • Three
- One
- Two
- Three

nested list in context¶

this is line before nested list, use two space indent to create sublist

  • One
    • One One
    • One Two
    • One Three
  • Two
    1. Two One
    2. Two Two
    3. Two Three
  • Three
    • Three One
      • Three One One
        • Three One One One

this is line after nested list

Blockquotes¶

> blockquote

Special Char Escapes¶

following characters need backslash escapes to generate literal characters.

\   backslash
`   backtick
*   asterisk
_   underscore
{}  curly braces
[]  square brackets
()  parentheses
#   hash mark
+   plus sign
-   minus sign (hyphen)
.   dot
!   exclamation mark
