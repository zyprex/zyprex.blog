Emacs eglot use jdtls on windows¶

CAUTION: the newest jdtls does not support java8.

Enviroment¶

  • windows10 Home 21H2
  • GNU Emacs 28.1 (build 2, x86_64-w64-mingw32)

Requirements¶

  • openjdk-8u41-windows-i586 ↗
  • jdt-language-server-0.55.0-20200430002 ↗

Extract ‘openjdk’ and ‘jdt-language-server’ somewhere, ensure ‘%JAVA_HOME%\bin’ is in PATH.

(with-eval-after-load "eglot"
  (add-to-list 'eglot-server-programs
               `(java-mode . ("java"
                              "-Declipse.application=org.eclipse.jdt.ls.core.id1"
                              "-Dosgi.bundles.defaultStartLevel=4"
                              "-Declipse.product=org.eclipse.jdt.ls.core.product"
                              "-Dlog.level=ALL"
                              "-noverify"
                              "-Xmx256m"
                              "-jar"
                              "c:\\SDK\\jdt-language-server-0.55.0-202004300028\\plugins\\org.eclipse.equinox.launcher_1.5.700.v20200207-2156.jar"
                              "-configuration"
                              "c:\\SDK\\jdt-language-server-0.55.0-202004300028\\config_win"
                              "-data"
                              jdtls-cahce-dir
                              ))))
