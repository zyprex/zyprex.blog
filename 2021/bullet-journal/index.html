BuJo aka. “ Bullet Journal ↗ ”, is a rapid logging technique.

Notations¶

symboldenotationexplain
●TasksTask incomplete
×TasksTask complete
>TasksTask Migrated into collection
<TasksTask scheduled into Future Log
-NotesThings you don’t want to forget
○EventsNoteworth moments in time
*SignifierHigh priority1
!SignifierGreat ideas, Inspiration

Start BuJo¶

I love to keep all things simplicity, i.e. KISS principle. So I only to choose part of core preception on BuJo rather than to use complete one.
I use five notations, “●”, “×”, “>”, “-”, “○”. Here are some reason why I turn down others.
The “<” may be forget after I complete the note, because each note should be ephemeral for my daily routines.
The “*” will aggravated my procarstination because it require extra energy (limited and precious willpower).
The “!”, not standout enough for a genius insight.
Some important rule:

  • only keep yesterday’s note.
  • before start today’s note, review yesterday’s note.
  • each note should be casually, it can’t do heavy tasks, just do micro tasks.
  • do not sad to your squiggle.

  1. “Use it sparingly. If everything is a priority, nothing is.” ↩︎
