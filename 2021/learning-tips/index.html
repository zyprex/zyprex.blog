Learning Tips
2021/02/14 Sun 09:33

Process learning¶

Focus on the process of learning and not the outcome, a product too far away to motivatie you. To optimize process learning, set a fixed study time in which you focus on the material for say 25 minutes a day. In that time, don’t try to reach a certain goal, just learn for the exact amount of time that you set yourself, then stop. Process learning will help you feel more relaxed during your study, and give you a sense of accomplishment once you are done.

13 tips¶

  1. Spaced repetition: study short but often, it is more productive to study regulary with sleeping breaks in between. Practice something for 15 minutes every day.
  2. Find your own style: watching videos, study with friends or study alone.
  3. Good night’s sleep: sleep and dreams are vital to processing and storing new information.
  4. Focus: protect yourself from distraction.
  5. Pomodoro technique: (for bord task only)
  6. Hard stuff first: control willpower
  7. Exercise, meditation and converse: grow your brain, lead to the creation of new neurons inside your brain and therefore grow its potential.
  8. Go places: create deeper memories by learning in a richer environment.
  9. Take fun seriously: find a fun way to practice.
  10. Space your studies: in order to remember things for a longer time, repeat the material in spaced intervals.
  11. 30% read 70% recite
  12. Instant self-test: after you study, finish up with a quick quiz. It’s much harder for your brain to reflect than to read, that extra effort creates deeper traces in your memory.
  13. Don’t force it: you cannot force yourself to be motivated just like you can’t tell someone else to be hungry.
